# About

I’m a marketing technology professional with a background in front-end engineering and design. Most recently at Annalect Australia, I led enterprise Tealium implementations across aviation, automotive and government clients. Prior to that, I spent four years at Tealium delivering implementations for more than 25 enterprise organisations.

Originally from Australia, I recently relocated to Nairobi, Kenya, where my partner is from. Outside of work, I spend most of my time with my one-year-old daughter, Coco. I enjoy working on home improvement projects, building practical tools, and staying active through tennis and jiu-jitsu.
